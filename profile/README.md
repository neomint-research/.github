# NeoMINT Research

> Open research for a safer digital world.

We're a Potsdam-based cybersecurity consultancy. In every client engagement — ISO 27001, BSI IT-Grundschutz, NIS 2 — we build tooling and frameworks to solve the problem in front of us. When that work can help the wider community, we publish it here.

This organization is our **research arm**: the repositories you find are the by-product of real projects, refined until they're useful on their own.

## What we publish

| Track | Focus |
| --- | --- |
| **Security tooling** | Small, focused utilities that solve recurring problems we hit in audits, assessments, and incident work. |
| **Compliance research** | Reference implementations and worked examples for ISO 27001, BSI IT-Grundschutz, and NIS 2. |
| **AI research lab** | Exploratory agents and pipelines — what AI can and cannot do safely inside a security-reviewed workflow. |

## Browse the work

The repositories below are what we've felt comfortable releasing so far — a mix of narrow utilities, compliance references, and early-stage AI experiments. Pinned items are the ones we actively maintain; the rest is there if it's useful to you.

## How we work

- **Research is a practice, not a department.** Every engineer is a consultant and every consultant ships code.
- **Small, legible projects.** We'd rather publish something narrow that works than something broad that almost does.
- **No growth at any cost.** We only scale if the work stays high-trust.
